Measurements

Measurements are essential for scientific research and experiments. They help quantify physical properties and allow for comparisons.

History of Measurements

History of Measurements in Chemistry

Ancient Times

Early humans used body parts for measurements (foot, hand, etc.).

This was the start of measurements, relying on personal scales.

16th Century

Creation of standardized units by the metric system.

The metric system aimed to unify measurements across Europe.

19th Century

Development of modern chemical measurements and instruments.

Instruments like the balance and thermometer were refined.

20th Century

Advancements in technology, leading to precise measurements.

Digital technology transformed measurement accuracy.
